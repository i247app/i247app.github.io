The Tale of iBi, the Unicorn Worm

In the magical land of Cryptopia, there lived a tiny green worm named iBi. With a sparkling unicorn antenna and a bright red tip, iBi had one wish: to bring happiness to the world.

Legend says whoever found iBi would be filled with endless joy and laughter. So, iBi became a symbol of happiness, wiggling through the cryptoverse, spreading smiles wherever it went.
