The Tale of ibi, the Unicorn Worm

In the magical land of Cryptopia, there lived a tiny green worm named ibi. With a sparkling unicorn antenna and a bright red tip, ibi had one wish: to bring happiness to the world. Legend says whoever found Ibi would be filled with endless joy and laughter. So, ibi became a symbol of happiness, wiggling through the cryptoverse, spreading smiles wherever it went.
